Will Kramer

Will Kramer

I am a Data Scientist with experience in Financial Services & Consulting. I enjoy using data to tell stories, facilitate change and develop products. I have a BS in Finance from Bentley University and a MS in Analytics from Georgia Institute of Technology. 

Data Science

I enjoy all things Data Science. The field is ever changing and I love the breadth and necessity for constant growth. I love searching for novel implementations of Data Science in the real-world. For me, it is a lifelong skill that I hope to continue to develop and refine throughout my career.   

Data Visualization

I enjoy being able to facilitate the transformation of advanced analytical findings and products into easy to understand visualizations. My customers have ranged from technical to non-technical, from fellow analysts to senior leadership.  

Sports Analytics

As a former collegiate athlete, I love when athletics and analytics intersect. Many of my side projects focus on intertwining these two subjects. Finding ways to creatively apply statistics or data science within the landscape of sports is exceptionally interesting to me.  

Project Work

I often use my free time to continiously improve my Data Science skills. I enjoy using competitions like Kaggle as frameworks to refine certain skills. For example, several of the notebooks shown on this site are not necessarily the best answer to a particular problem, but rather an effort to further develop a particular skillset within data science. Other academic and professional work has covered image classification, equity trading and market simulation and real estate property valuation.
